⚡ Critical "Grade A" Advice for Google Ads
Since you are running Google Ads to this new /book-a-tour page, you need to track when a booking actually happens.

The Blind Spot: The HubSpot embed is an iframe. Google Ads often cannot see when a user finishes a booking inside that iframe.

The Fix:

Go to your HubSpot Meeting Settings.

Find the "Redirect" option (What happens after they book?).

Create a simple page in Replit called /tour-confirmed.

Set HubSpot to redirect to 355main.com/tour-confirmed.

Tell your Google Ads manager: "Set the conversion goal to trigger when a user lands on the /tour-confirmed URL."

This is the only way to get accurate ROI data on your ad spend.
